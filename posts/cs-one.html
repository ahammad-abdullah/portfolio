How We Scaled an E‑commerce Store by 300% in 6 Months

June 10, 2026  |  5 min read  |  E‑commerce, Growth

The Challenge

Our client, a mid‑sized online retailer, was struggling with stagnant traffic, low conversion rates, and an outdated checkout experience. Their platform was slow on mobile devices, and abandoned carts were piling up. They needed a complete overhaul to compete in a crowded market.

Our Approach

We started with a thorough audit of their existing site, user behaviour, and technical performance. Then we implemented three key strategies:

  • Performance optimization: Migrated to a headless architecture + edge caching.
  • UX redesign: Simplified checkout from 5 steps to 1, added guest checkout, and improved mobile navigation.
  • Marketing automation: Set up abandoned cart emails, personalised product recommendations, and retargeting campaigns.

The Results

Within six months, the client saw:

  • +300% increase in overall revenue
  • +45% conversion rate on mobile devices
  • 58% reduction in cart abandonment
  • Page load speed improved from 4.2s to 0.9s

Key Takeaways

Investing in performance and user experience pays off dramatically. A fast, seamless checkout is no longer a luxury – it’s a necessity. And with the right automation, you can recover lost sales without extra ad spend.
